### 你的算法，就是你的命运
### Your Algorithm is Your Fate

这本书名为《写给未来决策者的算法》，但实际上，它讨论的是**“如何定义你自己”**。
This book is titled "Algorithm for Future Leaders," but in reality, it discusses **"How to Define Yourself."**

*   如果你遇到挫折就彻底放弃，你的算法里缺少了 **Momentum (动量)**。
    *   If you give up completely after a setback, your algorithm lacks **Momentum**.
*   如果你总是重复犯同样的错误，那是你的 **Backpropagation (反向传播)** 机制断裂了。
    *   If you keep making the same mistakes, your **Backpropagation** mechanism is broken.
*   如果你总是纠结于细枝末节而忽略大局，那是你的 **Attention Mechanism (注意力机制)** 权重分配错了。
    *   If you always obsess over trivial details and ignore the big picture, your **Attention Mechanism** has assigned the wrong weights.
*   如果你在舒适区里停滞不前，那是因为你的 **Learning Rate (学习率)** 衰减到了 0，或者你陷入了 **Local Minima (局部最优)** 却不敢引入一点 **Stochastic (随机性)** 来跳出去。
    *   If you stagnate in your comfort zone, it's because your **Learning Rate** has decayed to 0, or you are stuck in a **Local Minima** and dare not introduce a little **Stochasticity** to jump out.

在这个 AI 崛起的时代，甚至连机器都在努力地模仿人类大脑的运作机理（神经网络）。如果我们自己反而退化成了机械执行指令的机器，那才是最大的讽刺。
In this era of rising AI, even machines are striving to mimic the mechanism of the human brain (Neural Networks). It would be the ultimate irony if we ourselves regressed into machines that simply execute instructions.

**人之所以为人，不在于计算的速度（那是 GPU 的事），而在于定义 Loss Function（价值观）的能力。**
**What makes us human is not the speed of computation (that's for GPUs), but the ability to define the Loss Function (Values).**

机器可以极快地通过梯度下降逼近你设定的目标，但唯有你能决定：**我们到底要去哪座山顶？**
Machines can approach the goal you set extremely fast via gradient descent, but only you can decide: **Which summit are we actually trying to reach?**
